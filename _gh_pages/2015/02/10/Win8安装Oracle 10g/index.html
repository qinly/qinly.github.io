Win8安装Oracle 10g

10 February 2015

只要在Oracle 10G\database\stage\prereq\db\refhost.xml后面添加：

<CERTIFIED_SYSTEMS>
<!--Microsoft Windows 7  、Windows 8-->
<OPERATING_SYSTEM>
	<VERSION VALUE="6.1"/>
</OPERATING_SYSTEM>
<OPERATING_SYSTEM>
	<VERSION VALUE="6.2"/>
</OPERATING_SYSTEM>
<!--Microsoft Windows 7  6.1  、Windows 8  6.2-->
